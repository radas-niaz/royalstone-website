About Royal Stone Countertops & Blinds

Premium craftsmanship in stone surfaces and custom window coverings — proudly serving Winnipeg & surrounding areas.

Who We Are

Royal Stone Countertops & Blinds is a locally owned company specializing in the design, fabrication, and installation of high-quality quartz, granite, and marble countertops — and custom window coverings including zebra/dual, roller & blackout, and cellular blinds.

We handle every step in-house: precise templating, CNC fabrication, and meticulous installs for stone; professional in-home measurement, product selection, and clean, warrantied installs for blinds. One team, consistent quality.

Whether you’re renovating or building new, we bring samples to you, provide honest recommendations, and make sure your surfaces and shades look incredible and perform for years.

Why Homeowners Choose Us

  • Craftsmanship — seam-smart layouts, polished edges, and tidy caulking.
  • Blinds Expertise — zebra/dual, roller & blackout, cellular; manual or motorized.
  • End-to-End — measure → fabricate → install by one accountable team.
  • Peace of Mind — hardware & workmanship warranty; insured, respectful crews.
